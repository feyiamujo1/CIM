About Us

Clean Image Limited is a marketing, communications, and advertising consultancy firm with vast experience in organizing major sporting events, with football serving as one of our main areas of focus.

Our core values stand on the premise of creating robust platforms for rapid transformation of sport through global partnership and development of sustainable projects for sports development for nations and millions of people across the globe.

We create commercial opportunities for optimum brand maximization, marketing communication, research and development, technical assistance, grassroots development, elite sports commercialization, content design, training and corporate identity management.
